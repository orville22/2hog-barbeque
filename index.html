About Us

Our Story

We started 2hog Barbeque in 2020 out of a passion for delivering unparalleled Filipino street food experiences, where cleanliness is our utmost priority. Like all street food stalls, we also humbly began our journey on the streetside, inspired by the vibrant atmosphere and the opportunity to share our passion for the flavors we grew up with. As we witnessed the enthusiasm and joy our dishes brought to customers, we realized the immense potential to create a remarkable dining experience that embodies the essence of Filipino street food culture.

We wanted you to enjoy the authentic "isaw", "adidas", "betamax", etc. without any concerns because, at 2hog, we meticulously ensure the highest standards of hygiene and quality for your complete satisfaction.

By the way, for those who are unfamiliar, the term "2hog" or "Tuhog" refers to the art of skewering or the method of placing meat on a stick.
